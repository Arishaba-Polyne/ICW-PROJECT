Pro-Tips for the perfect Rolex

  1. **The flip Timing:** Don't flip the egg or chapati too early.Wait until the egg is almost completely set underneath before flipping.
  2. **Tight Roll:** Roll it while it is still warm and flexible to keep it tightly packed and prevent the omlet from tearing.
  3. **Add heat:** For an authentic Ugandan kick,mix in a pinch of fresh green chili or a dash of black pepper into your egg mixture
