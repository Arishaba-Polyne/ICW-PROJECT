The Authentic Ugandan Rolex

A beloved street food and national favorite! This simple,svory wrap combines a soft chapati with a fluffy vegetable omlet,creating a quick satisfying meal that's perfect anytime of the day.

Prep Time:10mins Cook Time:15mins Servings: 2mins

Quick Instructions

  1. ** Prep:** Whisk the eggs with onion, tomato, salt,pepper in a bowl.
  2. **Cook:** Pour mixture into a pan,lay the chapati on top and flip once the egg is mostly set.
  3. **Roll and serve:** Roll the chapati and omlet tightly together.Enjoy!
