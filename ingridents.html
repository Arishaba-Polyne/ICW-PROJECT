Full Ingriedents Lists

Essentials

  • 1 chapati
  • 2 Large Eggs
  • 1/4 Small Onion
  • 1/2 Medium Tomato
  • 1/4 Green Bell Pepper
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp Cooking Oil
  • A dash of fresh coriander,chopped(optional garnish)

Substitutions and Notes

For a healthier option, you can replace the cooking oil with a light spray of avocado oil. Feel free to add chili flakes or any desired vegetables like carrots or cabbage.
